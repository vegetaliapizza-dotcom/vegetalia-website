Frequently Asked Questions

Are all Vegetalia pizzas 100% pure veg?
Yes. Every single pizza at Vegetalia is 100% pure veg — from the dough to the sauce to the cheese and toppings. We are a dedicated pure veg kitchen. There is no meat, no egg, and no animal-derived ingredient used anywhere in our kitchen.
What is Vegetalia's pizza crust made of?
Our pizza crust is made from 100% whole wheat flour — no maida, no refined flour. It is thin, crisp and made fresh daily. Whole wheat gives you a healthier base without compromising on taste.
What is vegetarian mozzarella cheese?
Traditionally mozzarella cheese is made using animal rennet — an enzyme derived from the stomach lining of calves. This makes it non-vegetarian. At Vegetalia, we make our mozzarella in-house using vegetarian rennet derived from plant or microbial sources. So our cheese is 100% pure veg and safe for vegetarians and Jains. So now you know that it's not just the toppings that are pure veg — but also the cheese beneath it is pure veg.
Which areas in Bangalore does Vegetalia deliver pure veg pizza to?
We deliver pure veg pizzas to Hosakerehalli, Banashankari 2nd Stage, Banashankari 3rd Stage, Kathriguppe, Padmanabhanagar, Girinagar, Srinagar, Basavanagudi, Ittamadu, Uttarahalli, Kanakapura Road and BSK 2nd Stage BDA Complex.
How is Vegetalia different from mainstream pizza chains?
Mainstream pizza chains use maida (refined flour) for their base. At Vegetalia, every pizza is made on a whole wheat crust with no maida, and topped with in-house pure veg mozzarella made using veg rennet. We are a dedicated pure veg kitchen — not a mixed kitchen with a veg filter.
Can I order a Jain pizza with no onion and no garlic?
Yes. We have a dedicated No Onion No Garlic pizza on our menu. The sauce and all toppings are made without any onion or garlic. It is available in Regular 8 inch and Medium 11 inch sizes.
