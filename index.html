Frequently Asked Questions

We offer two sizes — Medium (11 inches, 8 slices, serves 2 people) and Regular (8 inches, 4 slices, serves 1 person).

Our pizza base is made from 100% whole wheat flour — no maida, no refined flour. It is thin crust, crisp and made fresh daily. Whole wheat gives you a healthier base without compromising on taste.

Whole wheat flour retains the bran and germ of the grain, making it higher in fibre and nutrients compared to refined maida. It gives our crust a slightly nutty flavour and a satisfying crisp bite — without the heaviness of a maida base.

Traditionally mozzarella cheese is made using animal rennet — an enzyme derived from the stomach lining of calves. This makes it non-vegetarian. At Vegetalia, we make our mozzarella in-house using vegetarian rennet derived from plant or microbial sources. So our cheese is 100% pure veg and safe for vegetarians and Jains.

Every Vegetalia pizza is triply loaded — three times the vegetable toppings you'd expect at a typical pizzeria. No sparse toppings, no disappointment. Just generous, fresh vegetables on every single slice.

Absolutely. Vegetalia is a pure veg kitchen — no meat, no eggs, no non-vegetarian ingredients of any kind. From the base to the toppings to the cheese, everything is 100% pure veg.

Yes! We have a dedicated No Onion No Garlic pizza on our menu — perfect for Jain customers and anyone who prefers to avoid onion and garlic. Both the pizza sauce and the toppings are completely free of onion and garlic. Same whole wheat crust, same in-house vegetarian cheese, same triply loaded fresh toppings — just without the onion and garlic. 100% Jain-friendly, 100% delicious.
